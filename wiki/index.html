About Chronicles of Eldoria: Nature's Trial
Prepare for an epic adventure in the wildest gaming wilderness! Survive, thrive, and gather precious gemstones to construct the ultimate portal and escape. Battle fierce creatures, master survival, and embark on a journey that's as catchy as your favorite tune. Get ready to write your own gaming legend!
Player
You, the master of memes and the avatar of adventure, the main character of this epic journey. Get ready to LOL your way through the untamed wilderness, crafting, battling, and exploring like a true gaming legend!
Control
  • Movement: W, A, S, D
  • Equip Item: E OR Left Mouse OR Up Arrow
  • Use Item: Space OR Left Mouse
  • Throw Item: Q OR Right Mouse OR Down Arrow
  • Change Active Inventory Slot: Alpha Keys OR Mouse Scroll Wheel OR Left and Right Arrow
  • Crafting: Tab
Materials
These materials are the lifeblood of your crafting endeavors. Gather resources like wood, stone, ores, and gems to fuel your creativity and unlock the potential of your crafting stations. Explore, mine, and harvest to collect the source of raw crafting materials and shape your world.
Food sources
In the wild, nourishment comes in various forms. Hunt animals, gather herbs, and forage for berries and fruits to fill your belly and sustain your adventures. Survival in the untamed wilderness demands resourcefulness and a keen eye for nature's bounty.
Tools
From the humble wooden sword to the mighty iron pickaxe, these tools are your companions in the untamed wilderness. Each serves a unique purpose, whether it's fending off enemies, harvesting resources, or shaping your world. Equip wisely, for your journey depends on the strategic deployment of these essential tools.
Gems
A shimmering repository of gemstones, each one a key to unlocking the portal leading back to the homeworld. Gather them all here and prepare for your triumphant return.
Crafting
These crafting stations are your personal workshop for forging your in-game destiny. Each one grants access to unique recipes, but remember, you'll need the right ingredients in your inventory to craft your next adventure.
